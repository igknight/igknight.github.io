What is igKnight?

The igKnight project is a scholarship competition designed to help students learn how to do research in life-sciences. This is an intense program lasting over a period of 3-4 months. The scholars will learn how to read research literature, write a research proposal and how to present their findings. The program also focuses on professional development tasks. After going through this program, we hope to train students to understand the connection between science and society, creatively solve problems and have an eye for the commercial impact of their work.
