Phase 1

This program is only open to second or third year students. We need to make sure that the people applying to this competition know this. Phase 1 is being used to collect basic information about candidates. After Phase-1 closes, during the screening period, we will select candidates to become part of igKnight as scholars if they meet selection criteria. There is only one criteria: Be a second year or early third year full-time student at UCF.
